Post Pandemic Work Life Balance

How Is Remote Work Affecting Our Well-Being?

The COVID-19 pandemic reshaped the way we work—bringing remote and hybrid setups into the mainstream. But years later, concerns linger: Has this shift improved our quality of life, or created new health challenges?

This project explores insights from the Remote Work Health Impact Survey (June 2025), a global dataset capturing how different work arrangements are influencing employees’ mental health, burnout, work-life balance, and social connection across industries, roles, and regions.
